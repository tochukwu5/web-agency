Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I get started with your web design services?

    Getting started is simple! Reach out to us via our contact page or give us a call. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project, goals, and requirements, and provide you with a tailored plan moving forward

  • How do I know nazdav can help me?

    You don't, and most often, neither do I. At least until I get some of your project details, the more the better. If I can't help you, or need to bring in additional help, I'll share that in your estimate- 100% obligation free.

  • How long does it take to design and develop a website?

    The timeline can vary based on the complexity and features of the website. A simple website might take a few weeks, while more intricate projects may take a couple of months. We work closely with you to establish a realistic timeline and keep you informed throughout the process.

  • Can you help with website maintenance and updates?

    Certainly! We offer ongoing website maintenance packages to keep your site running smoothly. From updates and security patches to content updates, we ensure your website remains up-to-date and secure.

  • How involved will I be in the design process?

    Your involvement is crucial! We collaborate closely with you to understand your vision and goals. Your feedback and input guide the design and development process to ensure the final result aligns with your expectations.

  • How much does a typical project cost?

    This is tough to ballpark- as a project varies based on the scope of work. A one page design -> dev project will be less than a 100+ page migration. Submit a budget with your request, and I'll let you know if I can work with it. Alternatively, you can contract me hourly.

  • Do you perform hourly work?

    Absolutely! This is my bread and butter. Rates vary depending the complexity of the task(s) and the proximity to a deadline or urgency of each request.

  • Who are the developers?

    As it stands, nazdav is a proud company of one. From start to finish you'll work with one point of contact, which means you can get the job done without stumbling through gobs of red tape.

  • What services does NazaDav offer?

    We provide a comprehensive range of services including website design, development, responsive design, e-commerce solutions, graphic design, user experience optimization, and more. Our goal is to create impactful online experiences tailored to your brand's needs.
